Research Testing Structures

With years of experience in designing and implementing custom testing structures for academic research, I specialize in integrating complex electrical systems and ensuring the safety of high-temperature and high-pressure plumbing infrastructure. My expertise allows me to create reliable, innovative solutions tailored to the unique needs of each project.

Commercial Electrical Drafting

During my undergraduate years, I worked part-time under a certified engineer, drafting electrical drawings that highlight my proficiency in CAD and adherence to NFPA code compliance. Additionally, I developed an electric panel load calculator in Excel, designed to assist my colleagues in accurately calculating electrical loads for panel design.

3D Design and Prototyping

As a personal hobby, I explore 3D design and prototyping, utilizing AutoCAD and Blender to create both functional items and artistic pieces. I design models, 3D print prototypes, and refine them through iteration until achieving the desired outcome. This process allows me to blend creativity with technical precision.

Welding
